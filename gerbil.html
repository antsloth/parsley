Home

Welcome to Restructure Management Services, Inc. We specialize in handling delinquent account receivables for our consumers while bringing dignity and integrity to the debt repayment process. Our mission is to assist individuals willing to recover their debts and rebuild their FICO credit scores by any legal amenable means, in order to help them get their lives and finances back on track.

It is our belief that the keys to success in any business endeavor include honesty, professionalism and a sound working knowledge of the issues affecting the customers we serve. To this end, we are constantly striving towards high standards of excellence. Our staff has decades of experience in this industry, so they are firmly committed to a sense of decorum and respect in every interaction.

We also faithfully observe the tenets set forth by the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act. The debt recovery process unfortunately often devolves into a stressful and negative experience due to the very nature of the business involved. However, we take pride in setting ourselves apart from others. Our company is always aspiring to deliver a positive experience in assisting our consumers through cordial and effective communication during every stage of the process.
